Expertise

Machine design expertise

Mitech has developed equipment for a wide range of clients, including: OEM manufacturers, R&D firms, consumer product manufacturers, government and defense contractors, as well as entrepreneurs developing new and innovative products. Specific technology areas and industries we have served include:

  • OEM and consumer product manufacturing
  • Alternative energy generation and use
  • Defense systems and related equipment
  • Marine and Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV) systems
  • Medical equipment and products
  • Hydraulic and pneumatic components and systems
  • Equipment for harsh and hazardous environments
  • New development and prototypes.

Client-centered engineering services

Our typical contributions include:

  • Developing new and/or novel products and equipment
  • Designing and fabricating equipment, fixtures, and tooling for regular product changes and additions
  • On-site troubleshooting, debugging, and/or upgrades of existing hardware
  • Providing experienced machine design capabilities to cover peak resource needs
  • Expanding product and service offerings
  • Increasing productivity and quality
  • Increasing reliability and reducing down time
  • Reducing production cost and cutting waste
  • Reducing operator repetitive injuries by incorporating human factors engineering into our designs.
