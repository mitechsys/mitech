--- title: Lagoon Pumping System layout: project ---

Developed in close collaboration with the customer, this system provides stand-alone suction and pumping of lagoon captured animal waste and provides sufficient capacity and pressure output to support direct pumping for remote toolbar application. System uses a high horsepower engine with a driveline to a submerged waste pump. Custom front axle design supports tight turns when towing on the road. Mitech activities included 3D CAD layout, drawing production and extensive kinematic and structural analysis.
