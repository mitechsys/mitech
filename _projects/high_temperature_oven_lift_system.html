--- title: High Temperature Oven Lift System layout: project text: This oven lift system was developed to handle 900-1000 °F oven temperatures. The system operates in an extremely dirty environment with all drive and actuation components located outside the oven. The wheeled dolly can be removed from under the product cart during the heating cycle to avoid damaging wheel bearings and wheels.
